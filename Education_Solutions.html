PGS AI Lab – Next-Gen Robotics & AI Innovation Hub Where Students Build the Future, Today

The PGS AI Lab is a future-ready innovation space that immerses students in AI, Robotics, and Automation through hands-on learning. Designed for schools, polytechnics, and colleges, the lab features plug-and-play tools and a ready curriculum to turn imagination into intelligent solutions. What’s Inside the Lab :

  • AI Dog Robot – A four-legged smart robot with gesture and voice control for exploring AI in motion and interaction.
  • Self-Driving Car Kits – Mini autonomous vehicles with sensors and vision AI for learning object detection and path planning.
  • Modular Robot Kits – Build-your-own robots like robotic arms, obstacle-avoiders, and line-followers for all skill levels.
  • Generative AI Stations – LLM-based tools for creating chatbots, AI art, and personal AI assistants.
  • Vision & Edge AI Tools – Face recognition, object tracking, IoT-based control systems, and automation projects.
  • Real-World Application Projects – Students build solutions like smart waste sorters, emotion detectors, crop monitoring tools, robotic assistants, and disaster-response bots.
  • Outcome: A hands-on tech hub where students learn, innovate, and solve real-world problems using AI and robotics.
